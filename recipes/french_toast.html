French Toast

Description

A classic dish that's easy to make. Tasty with butter and syrup

Ingredients

  • 6 slices of bread
  • 2 Eggs
  • 2/3 cup of milk
  • 1 teaspoon of vanilla extract and 1/4 teaspoon of cinnamon
  • Salt to taste
  • 1 tablespoon of butter

Steps

  1. Whisk together the eggs, milk, vanilla, cinnamon and salt in a bowl
  2. Lightly butter a griddle or skillet over a medium-high heat
  3. Soak the bread slices evenly in the egg mixture
  4. Take your soaked bread slices and place in the griddle/skillet and cook until golden.
  5. Serve hot with syrup
