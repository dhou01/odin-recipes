Lasagna

Description

A simple lasagna recipe everyone should try

Ingredients

  • Ground beef
  • Italian sausage
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato paste
  • Sugar
  • Seasoning: fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, fennel seeds and black pepper
  • Cheese: parmesan, mozzarella and ricotta
  • Lasagna sheets
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Prepare the meat sauce
  2. Cook the lasagna sheets and leave on the side
  3. Make the ricotta mixture
  4. Start layering the lasagna as follows: meat sauce, sheets, ricotta mixture, mozzarella slices, meat sauce, parmesan cheese and repeat
  5. Bake in the oven for about 50 minutes at 375 degrees
