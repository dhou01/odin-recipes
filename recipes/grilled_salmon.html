Grilled Salmon

Description

A nice and quick healthy meal that only takes 30 minutes

Ingredients

  • Salmon fillet
  • 1/3 cup of soy sauce
  • 1/3 cup of brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup of water
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Season the salmon fillet with salt, pepper
  2. In a bowl place the soy sauce, brown sugar, water and vegetable oil together and stir until the sugar is dissolved
  3. Place the salmon fillet in the sauce mixture and let it rest for at least 1 hour
  4. Preheat the oven for a medium-high heat
  5. Place the salmon fillet on an oven tray and cook for around 15 minutes.
  6. Serve with rice and/or with vegetables
